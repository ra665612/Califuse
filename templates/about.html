Hello and welcome to Califuse! I'm Ryan and I am the founder of the student network Califuse.com. I started Califuse to help students like myself who may have trouble connecting with companies that they want to work with. The inspiration for this network came when I noticed that the process in which companies connect with students in their industry is fundamentally flawed. Califuse believes that students are more than a simple resume and a GPA. Califuse allows students to share and present projects that they have either worked on in the classroom or independently. Califuse is a growing company currently in beta, and I would love for all of you to join our growing community and follow us as OUR network continues to grow and expand! Thank you.
